00060  * @brief Holder to implement conversion copies for @c DataVector/@c DataList.
00061  *
00062  * In some cases, we want to convert @c DataVector<A> to @c DataVector<B>,
00063  * where the two @c DataVector classes are not related by inheritance,
00064  * but where @c A and @c B are.  In such a case, we can make a new view
00065  * container for @c DataVector<B> and fill it with the pointers
00066  * from @c DataVector<A> (suitably converted).  In order for this
00067  * to work with the conversion machinery in SGTools, we must be able

00069  * Thus, we build a registry of @c DVLInfo objects, indexed
